Taco Bell's XXL Chalupa

Djangelic

Wow... so yesterday I went to Taco Bell for a quick dinner since we had church. I pulled into the driveway and saw a new item on the menu... the taco bell XXL chalupa... I was really impressed! It looked humongous! I order my food, and add the XXL to the order. Compared to Taco Bells  other offerings, its was pricey, costing $2.79 a piece. Since it was a one time thing, I didn't mind paying a bit more for it. I get home and open it, and lo and behold, i find that monster you see to the left. It was huge! I was impressed! It actually looks bigger out of the box, because it sinks into the box, as it is quite deep. Needless to say, it didnt last long. I devoured that thing like it was on sale! Taste wise, it was your run of the mill chalupa, but  quantity wise, it really fills you up. When you have a giant fried taco shell of that magnitude, you'd have to pretty hungry to not get full on that thing. So, what is the final verdict on it? Its a ginourmous chalupa. Yeah, thats it. You could order 2 chalupas or 1 XXL chalupa and get the same thing. I guess I shouldn't have been surprised... :)
